# 🧪 Microbial Growth Kinetics Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the growth behavior of mixed microbial cultures under different substrate concentrations. The study helps understand how microorganisms respond to varying nutrient conditions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Study microbial growth using **Optical Density (OD)**
* Measure **wet weight and dry weight biomass**
* Analyze growth trends under different glucose concentrations
* Understand basic **growth kinetics**

---

## 🧫 Methodology

1. Prepared synthetic wastewater with different glucose concentrations (5%, 10%, 15%)
2. Inoculated mixed microbial culture
3. Measured:

   * Optical Density (OD)
   * Wet weight
   * Dry weight
4. Recorded data at regular time intervals
5. Compared growth patterns across conditions

---

## 📊 Parameters Used

* Optical Density (OD)
* Wet Biomass
* Dry Biomass
* Substrate Concentration

---

## 🧠 Key Learnings

* Relationship between substrate concentration and microbial growth
* Importance of OD as a quick estimation tool
* Difference between wet and dry biomass analysis
* Basics of microbial growth kinetics
